Safe Travels

Here I stand on the edge
A crumbling cliff face ahead of me
A stray just off wind could snend me tumbling down

And as I look back up the hill
What once was a steep unending slope
seems like nothing but a molehill

Can I do it?
Can I still ventue forth?
What if I fall?
If I can't make it back?

I'm scared.



Homesickness

I felt homesick today
As if something were missing in my life
And yet it doesn't fade
No matter how hard I strife

I know what must be done
I know where to go
I should feel the sun
Under the old willow

Grounding my feet, I stretch my roots
Feeling my blood, anew, rebirthed
Into the sky, my flower shoots
I am at home with mother earth




Icarus

And before I knew it, I was flying
With you, it seemed anything was possible
Faster, higher, I flew more and more until
I was falling

Down, tumbling down
Falling faster and faster without restraint
My sun, how could you do this?
Falling for you




Lost & Found

there is a goddess in this small town
or perhaps she's but a lonely ghost
You can hear her in the streets, see her in the park
if you're lucky she'll talk to you before it's too late
she'll tell you to run as far as you can
and that you deserve better then what you have


but yet there's an ache in her voice
more a warning than a suggestion
not to make the same mistake, to wind up stuck
living forever in your tiny hometown
another voice lost, or possibly never found
