[for mills.txt] A Birthday Present



Chapter 0, introduction

hi hi milla !! its your birthday !!
well not actually... its august 22nd when i'm writing this.

regardless ! your birthday is coming up soon, and last year i promised that your gift would be something we can both enjoy.

so,, here i am writing up some nice words, hopefully you can read them again when the seasonal sad starts to get to you.

Chapter 1, One Year

It's almost been a year since we meet. August 24th, The Beths.
Or maybe it was earlier? I do not remember going to Stella's Birthday however..
Regardless, a year of talking to you, and wow.
On one hand, it's just one year;
on the other, one whole entire year.

This has been one of the smoothest friendships I've ever had.
It may sound like I'm repeating myself, but everything flows so naturally when talking to you.
I truly cannot wait to spend more years in your care.
And hopefully, hang out with you again in person.

Chapter 2, Affirmations(?)

Who doesnt like being showered in compliments? I hope you do, because that's what this section is.


You are one of the most genuinely kind people i know. Talking to-and being with-you feels like one is in a soft, safe space. Not many people can create such an authentic space, but you seem to be able to.

You have such a love and appreciation for all things beautiful that I have found myself slowing down and noticing more and more beauty in the world.


Of course I can mention how absolutely gorgeous and pretty you are, but that goes without saying.

Would much rather say that you've built such an amazing image of yourself as this outgoing, bubbly, and loving person.

Chapter 3, Ramblings

This is really just me putting words on a page, but we see the world in a similar way so you might enjoy it ^-^

One of the things that everybody talks about that I dont belive in is Falling In Love. My love has always been a choice, a commitment that I specifically want to make. Love to me is two people who share a connection (a spark, of sorts) and both make an effort to deepen that connection and to build on top of it.

I may be aromantic? I still want to spend the rest of my life with someone or someones, however.

Any romantic relationship I've had could've been a close friendship, and any close friendship a romantic relationship. The only difference is both parties making an agreement to deepen our connection.

I dunno, you may like the sentiment though.

Chapter 4, Excerpts from poetry

Lines from poetry books i have that i think you'll like!!

And speak my passion--heaven or hell?
...
-- Browning, "One Way Of Love"


Go sit upon the lofty hill,
And turn your eyes around,
Where waving woods and waters wild
Do hymn an autumn sound
The summer sun is faint on them-
The summer flowers depart-
Sit still-a all transformed to stone,
Except your musing heart.
...
-- E.B Browning, "The Autumn"

be rough and hiary? -- I'm glad they are like that.

Chapter 5, Conclusions

In conclusion, I love you so much. You truly are a blessing in my life, and not a day goes by that I'm not thankful for that.
I do feel sorry that I couldn't give you anything more significant, but sincerly hope that you've enjoyed what I have cobbled together.
I don't see us not being friends anytime soon; so when we reunite, lets celebrate both our birthdays in earnest.

Nonetheless I hope you have a wonderful day today, and that its only good vibes.
Happy Birtday Mills, rooting you on from the other side of the world.

Yours,
Lyla.
